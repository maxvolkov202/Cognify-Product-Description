# Cognify — Positioning & Copy

## One-line positioning

> Cognify is a communication training gym. Short, structured reps with instant feedback build the ability to think clearly and speak under pressure.

## The elevator pitch

Communication is the #1 most in-demand skill globally — yet fewer than half of graduates meet employer expectations. Cognify is the first system that actually trains it. Ten minutes a day, daily reps, instant feedback, measurable progress. The Duolingo for communication.

## The two-sentence differentiator

Other tools analyze how you sound or simulate one-off conversations. Cognify trains how you think — through short, framework-driven reps that compound into real skill.

## Dual-track messaging

v2 leads with enterprise per David's advice, keeps consumer as a lightweight second path, and never pretends they are the same audience.

### Enterprise L&D (primary)

**Hero H1**: *Train your team to think clearly under pressure.*
**Sub**: Cognify is a communication training system — daily reps, instant feedback, measurable progress — built for L&D teams who need to prove their training works.
**Primary CTA**: `Book a pilot`
**Secondary CTA**: `See a sample progress report`

**Enterprise narrative beats**
- The communication gap is real: 57% of leaders, 91% of employees, 86% of workplace failures.
- Existing tools are Grammarly for speech — they polish what's already there. Cognify builds the underlying skill.
- Measurability is the point. Per-seat dashboards, exportable progress reports, validated improvement receipts.
- Start focused (career centers, sales teams, customer-facing roles). Expand broadly.

### Individual / consumer (secondary, lightweight)

**Hero H1** (v1-preserved): *Train clear thinking into clear speech.*
**Sub** (v1-preserved): Cognify is a communication training gym where you practice real conversations out loud. Through short, structured reps and immediate feedback, you build the ability to organize thoughts and explain ideas clearly under pressure.
**Primary CTA**: `Start training free`
**Secondary CTA**: `See how the gym works`

**Consumer narrative beats**
- 75% of people fear public speaking. 45% pass up promotions because of it.
- Confident speakers are 70% more likely to be promoted.
- 10 minutes a day. Daily reps. Real improvement. Built like a gym, not a course.
- One rep closer to clarity.

## Stats bar (the marketing proof points)

Cycle these on the homepage and `/for-teams` page:

- **#1** — most in-demand skill globally (LinkedIn 2024)
- **91%** of employees say their managers lack effective communication skills
- **86%** of employees cite ineffective communication as the main cause of workplace failures
- **57%** of leaders cite communication skill gaps in their workforce
- **75%** of people fear public speaking
- **45%** pass up promotions because of it
- **70%** more likely to be promoted if confident in public speaking
- **$5.8B** communication training market → **$11.2B by 2034**

## Words we never use

- "AI coach" — sounds like a chatbot
- "Soft skills" — reductive, corporate
- "Public speaking" as the only use case — too narrow
- "Transform" — overused
- "Empower" — overused
- "Leverage" — overused
- "Unlock your potential" — Insta-caption energy

## Tone

Confident, direct, quietly technical. Short sentences. No exclamation points. Gym/training metaphor earned, not forced. The product is the proof — don't oversell.
