| 4 | Owen | §4.1 | Timing should be only ONE kind of "constraint". Others: Structure (STAR), Tone, Complexity (ELI5). "Explain X to a 12-year-old" vs "Explain X in 30s" train different skills; not all skilled communication is time-boxed. |
| 5 | Owen | §4.1 | "Progressive overload": repeated responses allow increased complexity/constraint. E.g. after "Pitch your idea to your colleague in 120s" → next rep raises stakes: "Pitch the same idea to your boss in 90s". |

| 7 | Owen | §4.1 | Build a Rep is vastly different from DW/Lab: far more user input, more complex feedback, more memory (longer responses), likely premium-tier due to memory/compute. |
